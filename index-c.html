The Story

Built In The 305

GwalaBoy Phlyy is a North Miami rapper building a loyal following with unapologetic delivery, street-rooted authenticity, and a relentless grind. He's watched the evolution of Miami's music scene firsthand — and carved his own lane through it.

Influenced by Gucci Mane, Future, and Lil Wayne, his sound reflects both his environment and his ambition. From North Miami Big Ape to the 8-track Going Colossal, he's earned co-signs and collaborations with Bobby Fishscale, Sauce Walka, Jackboy, and Fredo Bang — with a Rob49 link-up on the way.
